About the Craftsman

From a young age, I was fascinated by the beauty and potential locked within a simple piece of wood. My journey started in my grandfather's workshop, where I learned the value of patience, precision, and respecting the material.

Today, I run a small workshop dedicated to creating beautiful, functional, and enduring pieces of furniture. My design philosophy is rooted in clean lines, honest materials, and traditional joinery techniques. Each project is a collaboration with my clients to bring their vision to life.

When I'm not covered in sawdust, you can find me exploring the forests and mountains, forever inspired by the natural world.

Sapele Outdoor Furniture Set

A perfect blend of modern design and all-weather resilience.

Project Details

This three-piece set, consisting of two enveloping chairs and a matching loveseat, was designed to be the centerpiece of an outdoor living space. The frames are constructed from solid Sapele, a durable and weather-resistant hardwood known for its beautiful interlocking grain. Each joint is a robust mortise and tenon, ensuring the furniture will last for generations.

Materials & Finish

  • Solid Sapele Hardwood
  • Hard Wax Oil Finish for maximum protection and a natural feel
  • Marine-Grade, Antimicrobial Outdoor Fabric
  • Mildew and Mold-Resistant Reticulated Foam
